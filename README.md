## Physics



### momentum conservation


$$
 m_1 \vec{u_1} + m_2 \vec{u_2} = m_1 \vec{v_1} +m_2 \vec{v_2}
$$


### Energy conservation


$$

 \frac{1}{2}m_1{\vec{u_1}}^2 + \frac{1}{2}m_2{\vec{u_2}}^2 = \frac{1}{2}m_1{\vec{v_1}}^2 + \frac{1}{2}m_2{\vec{v_2}}^2
 
 $$



### coordinate
